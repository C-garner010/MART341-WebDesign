The end of the beginning

It's weird, coming to the end of things. I have so many more questions and only feel as if I've just begun. I am excited to see where I will go from here and have absolutely no plans of stopping. In fact, I have registered for creative coding next semester so I can pursue a certificate in web development. I am also excited to get to practice all that I have learned over the summer on a practice site of my own. I'm hoping I can make some serious progress before I take web technologies and web servers. I would like to figure out how to make room for class about illustrator and vector graphics, but only time and money will tell. So for now, I definitely have a greater and more fleshed out plan than before so I am excited!

Over the summer, my plan is to work on adding in more pages with proper links to support a rough attempt at a blog. I will also be able to allow myself a bit more time to write so when I decide to begin publishing, I will have a greater cushion and "backup" pool to pull from.

This "final" site of mine is actually the exact opposite, this is the beginning of myself being able to see that I can begin genuinely creating some of the things stuck within my mind. I know that I still have a ton to learn but I am grateful for that awareness now. I am grateful to finally have begun somewhere. There is no where to go but up now.
