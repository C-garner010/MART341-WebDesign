This has been a goal of mine for the past few years and so to actually begin and see tangible results is super encouraging. I am finding joy in figuring out the "pieces of the puzzle" per say. Well, relatively of course at this point, but, I'm far further than I was a year ago and that is more than I could ask for.

One of the minor details I have noticed to be comically frustrating is the change in my trying rate. I am a decent typist, I have hit over 200wpm before but definitly cannot yet keep that up here. And my spelling seems to be iffy to say the least and so it will be fun to see how my efficiency and competence evolves in this aspect.

I am beginning to see just how quickly this all becomes far more complex and know I cannot yet fully fathom some of those frustrations, however, I am notebly excited for that challenge in specific because I am finding that I really kinda enjoy it. I like creating an entire something.

I'm excited to dedicate my time toward refining my skills in this field. Even if this stayed a personal endevor (which I don't honestly see that being the case but that is one of my main driving forces), I am excited and grateful to have an opportunity to house a part of myself almost eternally. Ah, what a terrifying and yet exhilerating thought.
